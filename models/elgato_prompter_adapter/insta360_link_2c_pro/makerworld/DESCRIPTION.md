## 📦 What's in the Box

*   **Adapter Plate**: The main adapter that clips onto the Elgato Prompter and securely holds the Insta360 Link 2C Pro.
*   **Demo Piece**: A small, material-saving test piece to verify printer tolerances before printing the full plate.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kellerlabs/assets/main/homeracker/models/elgato_prompter_adapter/insta360_link_2c_pro/adapter_iso.jpg" alt="Adapter ISO View" width="800">

## 🛠️ Assembly & Customization Guide

The assembly is straightforward and requires no extra hardware.

> [!TIP]
> **Material Recommendation**: While PLA works fine, I highly recommend using a hard TPU like **TPU68D** (such as Bambu Lab TPU for AMS in black). The fit is just as tight, but the rubber-like nature makes it much less "scratchy" when inserting the camera, and it provides noticeably better grip.

### 1. Print the Demo Piece (Optional but Recommended)
The provided print profile includes both the full adapter plate and the small demo piece on separate plates. I highly recommend printing just the demo piece first. This helps you verify that your printer's tolerances allow the camera to fit snugly without being too tight or too loose.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kellerlabs/assets/main/homeracker/models/elgato_prompter_adapter/insta360_link_2c_pro/demo_piece-to_spare_material_and_try_fit.jpg" alt="Demo Piece" width="800">

If the fit isn't perfect, you can easily adjust it using the **MakerWorld Parametric Model Maker (PMM)**. Simply click "Customize" on the model page and adjust the `clearance` variable (default is `0.2`mm). You can also set `print_demo` to `true` to generate and download just the test piece before committing to the full print.

### 2. Insert the Camera
Aim the camera into the adapter hole from the front side.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kellerlabs/assets/main/homeracker/models/elgato_prompter_adapter/insta360_link_2c_pro/assembly_1_aiming_camera_into_hole_from_front.jpg" alt="Aiming camera into hole" width="800">

Firmly press the camera into the hole.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kellerlabs/assets/main/homeracker/models/elgato_prompter_adapter/insta360_link_2c_pro/aseembly_2_pressing%20camer%20into%20hole.jpg" alt="Pressing camera into hole" width="800">

Ensure the camera is fully inserted and flush with the adapter.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kellerlabs/assets/main/homeracker/models/elgato_prompter_adapter/insta360_link_2c_pro/assembly_3_camera_inserted.jpg" alt="Camera fully inserted" width="800">

Here is how it should look from the back once the camera is properly seated.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kellerlabs/assets/main/homeracker/models/elgato_prompter_adapter/insta360_link_2c_pro/assembly_4_back_view_including_inserted_camera.jpg" alt="Back view with inserted camera" width="800">

### 3. Attach to the Prompter
Hook the Bottom of the adapter into the back bracket of the Elgato Prompter.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kellerlabs/assets/main/homeracker/models/elgato_prompter_adapter/insta360_link_2c_pro/assembly_5_hook_in_adapter_to_prompter.jpg" alt="Hook in adapter to prompter" width="800">

Swing the adapter up to close the hole.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kellerlabs/assets/main/homeracker/models/elgato_prompter_adapter/insta360_link_2c_pro/assembly_6_closing%20the%20hole%20with%20the%20adapter.jpg" alt="Closing the hole" width="800">

Secure the adapter using the original Elgato thumbscrews on the left and right.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kellerlabs/assets/main/homeracker/models/elgato_prompter_adapter/insta360_link_2c_pro/assembly_7_screw_in_left_thumbscrew.jpg" alt="Screw in left thumbscrew" width="800">

### You're Done!
The camera is now perfectly aligned and ready for use.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/kellerlabs/assets/main/homeracker/models/elgato_prompter_adapter/insta360_link_2c_pro/assembly_8_back_view.jpg" alt="Completed back view" width="800">
